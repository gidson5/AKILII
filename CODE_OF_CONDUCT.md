# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Akili is built for MiniPay users across Africa. Everyone contributing to this project — regardless of background, experience, or location — deserves a respectful and welcoming environment.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in all communication
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Give credit where it is due
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Sharing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, email **akilihq12@gmail.com**. All reports are handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate this code of conduct.
